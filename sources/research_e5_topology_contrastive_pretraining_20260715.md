## E5 Use

Graph contrastive pretraining and topology corruption are occupied general ML
ideas. The candidate Innovation 1 contribution must therefore be narrower:
cipher-spec-generated typed SPN operators, a deterministic shuffled-permutation
counterfactual, strict differential-neural data, and a predeclared cross-SPN
one-epoch adaptation gate. The shuffled operator must remain an attribution
control, not an arbitrary augmentation tuned on GIFT validation performance.
